What is eDonation

eDonation is a web application that provide a platform to the organisations who helps the helpless poeple.
These Organisations Powered by the donations that are recieved by kindle people(donors).
This web application bring those organisations to you, if you want to help then you can donate them trhough eDonation.


How eDonation Works

There are two types of users in eDonation, first Donors who give donations and another one are called Organisations who recieved Donations
The organisations come to eDonation website and register them,
Then the organisations after registration the need to be verified by Administration for showing their link and information, also they can not recieved donations until verified.
After verified by Administration organisation link show to eDonation's webpage and donors can give donations to them.
